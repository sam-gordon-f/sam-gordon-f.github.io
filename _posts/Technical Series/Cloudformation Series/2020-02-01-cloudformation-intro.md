Its easy to produce a result in any of the major cloud via the console / API's; however creating it in a repeatable and documented fashion is what everyone should be striving to do!

Cloudformation is an incredibly powerful infrastructure templating service, and vital to at least understand in principal for anyone looking to keep their tech wealth in AWS.

It allows you to define desired state of your solutions and progressively apply updates / track your history of changes.
